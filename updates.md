## G. Add these FAQ answers

Your FAQ headings exist, but the answers need to sell activation confidence. ([ondial.me][1])

### Do I need a setup call?

No. Most clinics can complete setup asynchronously from the website import and setup chat. If you want help with forwarding or testing, we can guide you by email.

### What if my website is missing information?

That is normal. OnDial imports what it can from your site, then asks only for what is missing.

### Do I need a new phone number?

No. You can keep your current clinic number and forward calls when you want OnDial to answer.

### Can I review everything before going live?

Yes. Nothing goes live until you confirm the clinic profile, greeting, rules, and urgent-call handling.

### Does OnDial give medical advice?

No. OnDial is an AI receptionist, not a veterinarian. It follows your clinic’s instructions and directs urgent callers based on the guidance you define.

### Is this only for after-hours?

No. You can use it for after-hours only, overflow during busy times, or most inbound calls.

### What happens if I am not ready to finish setup?

You can save progress by email and return later.
