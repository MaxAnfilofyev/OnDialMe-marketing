# Recording setup

## Browser tabs to have open

1. Your landing page home
2. The setup chat modal
3. The prefilled review form
4. Stripe checkout or trial-start screen
5. Post-checkout “forward your number / test call” screen
6. One example clinic website for personalization in the cold follow-up video

## Recording settings

* Tool: Loom
* Camera: off
* Mic: on
* Screen: browser only
* Zoom: 110–125%
* Cursor: move slowly, click deliberately
* Pace: calm, slightly faster than normal conversation
* Total demo length: **1:50–2:20**
* Cold follow-up video length: **0:35–0:55**

## Demo account / sample clinic

Use one fictional or sanitized sample clinic:

* Clinic: “Greenwood Veterinary Clinic”
* City: “Raleigh, NC”
* Hours: Mon–Fri 8–6, Sat 9–1, Sun closed
* Appointment types: sick, wellness, vaccines, follow-up, new client
* Escalation: warm transfer
* SMS sender: Greenwood Vet

# 2) Cold email follow-up video

## Goal

Get a reply without a meeting.

## When to use it

Send this after:

* they opened but did not reply
* they replied with mild interest
* you want to push them from curiosity to “send me the draft”

## Assets to have on screen

1. Their clinic website homepage
2. Your setup chat or prefilled config draft
3. Their contact page or hours page
4. Your landing page CTA or draft review page

## Length

**40–50 seconds**

---

## Follow-up email text to send with the video

**Subject:** Quick personalized walkthrough for {{Clinic Name}}

**Body:**
Hi {{First Name or Team}},

I recorded a short walkthrough for {{Clinic Name}} so you can see how the setup would look using your public website information.

Video: {{loom_link}}

If you want, I can send the prefilled draft link and you can review it asynchronously.

Reply YES and I’ll send it.

– Max

---

# 3) Personalized placeholders for the cold follow-up video

Use these substitutions:

* `{{Clinic Name}}` = exact clinic name
* `{{First Name or Team}}` = named contact if you have one, otherwise “team”
* `{{one visible website detail}}` = hours, location, appointment request page, new client page, urgent care language, or services page

Example line:
“I can already see your weekday hours and your new client flow here, so those can be prefilled immediately.”

# 4) On-screen choreography

## Cursor behavior

* Keep it still while speaking full sentences
* Move only when changing sections
* Use short, clean hover motions
* Scroll in one direction only
* No rapid tab switching

## Page timing

* Hero: 10–15 sec
* Setup chat: 35–45 sec
* Review form: 25–35 sec
* Trial/activation: 20–25 sec
* Close: 10 sec

## Voice timing

* One sentence
* brief pause
* one action
* one sentence

That rhythm makes you sound controlled.

---

# 5) Exact recording checklist

## Before pressing record

* close distracting tabs
* disable notifications
* log into the demo environment
* prefill sample values you will use
* rehearse the clicks once
* put the script right next to the browser window

## While recording

* start with the landing page already loaded
* speak the first sentence before moving the cursor
* click only after the sentence that sets up the click
* keep the entire recording under 2:20

## After recording

* title the landing page demo:
  **2-minute overview: Vet AI Receptionist**
* title the personalized follow-up:
  **Quick draft walkthrough for {{Clinic Name}}**

# 6) The first sentence options for different situations

## General cold outreach follow-up

“Hi, this is Max. I wanted to make this concrete, so I recorded a quick walkthrough using your clinic’s public website.”

## After a reply like “sounds interesting”

“Hi, this is Max. Since you said this sounded interesting, I recorded a short walkthrough so you can see exactly how the setup would look for your clinic.”

## After no reply but repeated opens

“Hi, this is Max. I noticed you may have taken a look, so I made a quick clinic-specific walkthrough to show the setup more concretely.”

---

# 7) Best demo order for conversion

Record in this order:

1. generic landing page demo first
2. then 3–5 personalized follow-up videos for the top prospects

The generic video builds trust.
The personalized video gets the reply.

# 8) The CTA to use everywhere

Use the same CTA in the video and email:

**“Reply YES and I’ll send the draft link.”**

That is the right friction level for the first wave.
